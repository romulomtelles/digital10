eSIM Plans

eSIM plans are digital products delivered instantly by email. Because activation begins as soon as your QR code is issued, different rules apply compared to physical goods.

No returns or refunds on eSIM plans. All sales are final once the QR code has been delivered to your email. This applies to all plan types and durations.

📧 Reporting a Problem

  • Contact us while your plan is still active. Reports made after a plan has expired cannot be investigated or actioned — the window to resolve connectivity issues is while you still have an active subscription.
  • Email info@digital10.ca with your order number, the issue you're experiencing, and the country you're in.
  • We will work with our eSIM provider to investigate and, if the problem is on our end, issue a replacement plan or credit at our discretion.
  • Issues caused by your device not supporting eSIM, incorrect activation, or carrier restrictions in your destination are outside our control and do not qualify for compensation.

📱 Before You Buy

  • Verify that your device is eSIM-compatible and unlocked before purchasing. Most modern iPhones and Android flagships support eSIM — check your device settings.
  • Plans are data-only. Voice calls and SMS are not included unless explicitly stated in the plan description.
  • Coverage and speeds depend on local carrier agreements. Digital10 does not guarantee specific speeds in any country.
  • QR codes are delivered to the email address provided at checkout — double-check your email before completing payment.
  • Prices are charged in USD and displayed in CAD at the current exchange rate. Your bank may apply its own conversion fee.

⏱️ Daily Plans

  • Daily plans are priced per day. The number of days you select at checkout is what you will be charged for — no automatic renewal.
  • The plan validity period begins upon activation, not upon purchase.
Questions about your eSIM? Email info@digital10.ca with your order number and we'll respond within one business day.

  • Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer. We recommend using a tracked service — Digital10 is not responsible for items lost in return transit.
  • Once we receive and inspect the return, your refund (minus the restocking fee) will be processed within 5 business days to your original payment method.
  • Items returned after 30 days or without prior contact will not be accepted.

🛠️ Defective or Incorrect Items

  • If your item arrives defective, damaged, or not as described, contact us within 30 days of delivery and we will arrange a full refund or free replacement — no restocking fee applies.
  • Email info@digital10.ca with your order number and a description of the issue. Photos or a short video of the defect help us resolve things faster.

How to start a return: Email info@digital10.ca before sending anything back. We'll confirm eligibility and give you the return address. Items sent without prior authorization may be refused.

🛡️ 90-Day Warranty

  • During the warranty period, defective items will be repaired, replaced, or refunded at Digital10's discretion.
  • The warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, drops, liquid exposure, unauthorized modification, or normal wear and tear.
  • To make a warranty claim, email info@digital10.ca with your order number and a description of the fault.

🚫 Non-Returnable Items

  • Items that have been opened, assembled, soldered, or modified are not eligible for the standard return policy.
  • If such an item is defective, the warranty policy still applies.
