1 · Who we are

Digital10 is a Canadian business based in Moncton, New Brunswick, operating at digital10.ca. We sell web hosting plans, global eSIM data plans, and electronics components.

Digital10 is the data controller responsible for the personal information collected through this website. Questions about this policy can be directed to info@digital10.ca.

2 · What information we collect

We collect information only when you make a purchase or contact us. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics platforms, or advertising pixels.

Information Why it's collected
Full name To address your order and any correspondence
Email address To deliver your eSIM QR code, order confirmation, and support replies
Postal / ZIP code & country For payment fraud prevention (passed to Stripe)
Payment details Processed directly by Stripe — Digital10 never sees or stores your card number
Order details Product purchased, amount, date, and order number — kept for business and tax records
Shipping address Electronics orders only — required to ship your item

3 · How we use your information

  • Fulfilling your order — sending your eSIM QR code, shipping your electronics, or activating your hosting plan.
  • Order confirmation emails — a receipt is sent automatically after each successful payment.
  • Customer support — if you contact us, we use your information to help resolve your issue.
  • Legal and tax obligations — Canadian tax law requires us to retain transaction records.

We do not use your information for marketing emails, targeted advertising, or sell it to any third party.

4 · Third parties we share data with

We share the minimum necessary data with trusted service providers to operate the business. We do not sell your data.

Service Purpose Data shared
Stripe (stripe.com) Payment processing Name, email, postal code, country, payment card details
eSIM Access (esimaccess.com) eSIM plan provisioning and QR code generation Name, email, and the plan you purchased
Resend (resend.com) Transactional email delivery (order confirmations, QR codes) Name and email address

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy and is responsible for the data they hold. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns.

5 · How long we keep your data

  • Order records (name, email, product, amount) are retained for 7 years to comply with Canada Revenue Agency record-keeping requirements.
  • Support correspondence is kept for as long as reasonably necessary to resolve the matter, then deleted.
  • We do not retain payment card details — these are held solely by Stripe.

6 · Your rights

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information in your records.
  • Withdraw consent for uses beyond what is legally required to process your order (note: withdrawing consent may limit our ability to provide service).
  • Request deletion of personal data we hold, subject to our legal retention obligations.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@digital10.ca. We will respond within 30 days.

7 · Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified provider — Digital10 never handles raw card data.

Order data is stored on a private server with access restricted to Digital10 personnel. While we take reasonable precautions, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. If you have security concerns, contact us at info@digital10.ca.
